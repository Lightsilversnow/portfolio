C4 model application

Intro

To help us with organizing the different parts and pages of our desktop and mobile application, we (Tessa & Robin) made a C4 model. This model spans both the Desktop as well as the Mobile application in different sections.

Conclusion

Due to making the C4 model we now have a better overview of the application and the desktop variant. This also ensures that we won't miss a page when we're coding it.
